FAQs

Common questions

Do I need to be a D4Driving pupil to buy the course?
No — the course is available to any learner driver in the UK. D4Driving pupils simply get a 50% discount with the code provided by Robert at lessons.
How long do I have access for?
You get 12 months full access from the date of purchase. That's plenty of time to work through all the material at your own pace and revisit anything before your test.
Can I watch on my phone?
Yes — the course works on any device. Phone, tablet, laptop or desktop. Your progress is saved automatically so you can pick up where you left off.
I'm not taking my test in Peterborough — is it still worth it?
Absolutely. Modules 1–13 and 15 cover universal driving skills that apply to every test centre in the UK. The local route videos (Module 14) are a bonus — but the rest of the course stands fully on its own.
Is this a replacement for driving lessons?
No — and it's not meant to be. The course is designed to complement your lessons by giving you the theory knowledge before you get in the car. Learners who arrive prepared make faster progress and need fewer lessons.
What if I buy the wrong course?
Get in touch and we'll swap it. If you're unsure whether to go manual or automatic, the automatic course in the Yaris Cross is often the quicker route to test — but talk to Robert if you need advice.
